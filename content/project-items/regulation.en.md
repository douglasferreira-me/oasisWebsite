## Public information for better decisions

The project organizes information currently scattered across legislative, executive and judicial sources. Its purpose is to turn complex data and documents into accessible dashboards, timelines, infographics and reports.

The initiative follows debates in Brazil's National Congress, federal executive actions, court decisions and international references. It seeks to increase transparency and improve public participation in discussions involving artificial intelligence, digital platforms and fundamental rights.

Outputs will be open and documented, enabling researchers, organizations, public officials and society to follow trends, compare experiences and ask better-informed questions.
